TCS has launched an industry-relevant undergraduate engineering course called "Computer Science and Business Systems" (CSBS) in
partnership with leading academicians across India. The curriculum is designed to align technical education with human values and
address the critical need for qualified engineers with a humanistic approach. The CSBS program aims to equip students with the core
topics of Computer Science while also emphasizing humanities, management sciences, and human values.
The program offers exposure to cutting-edge technologies and incorporates experiential learning. It also focuses on ethics and life
values, ensuring that graduates not only possess technical skills but also understand the importance of applying them responsibly.
Thirty renowned educational institutes across India have signed Memorandum of Understandings with TCS to offer the CSBS program,
with RVR&JC being one of the pioneering institutes to initiate the program in 2019 with an intake of 64 students.
